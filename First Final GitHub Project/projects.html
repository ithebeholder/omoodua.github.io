Pac-Man Exercise

Pac-Man oringinally is the character in an action maze chase video game. In my Pac-Man project, I take four different images of Pac-Man and use them to create an animation of the Pac-Man running across the screen. Once Pac-Man gets to edge of the screen he turns around and begins traveling in the other direction. This project helped me learn how manipulate the DOM to change which images appear on the screen and where on the page they show up.
